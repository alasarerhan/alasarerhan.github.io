    Projects

    Multimodal Recommendation System

    The developed platform aims to provide personalized recommendations across various domains (Airbnb, movies, anime, games, books).

    By analyzing user preferences and behaviors, the system seeks to recommend the most relevant content.

    This project leverages a variety of machine learning and filtering techniques to improve user satisfaction and maximize user engagement on the platform.

    Armut Association Rule Learning

    Armut, an online service platform in Turkey, connects service providers and service recipients, offering easy access to services such as cleaning, renovation, and transportation via computers or smartphones. In this project, a product recommendation system was developed using Association Rule Learning, leveraging a dataset that includes services received by users and their corresponding categories.

    End to End ML with Scoutium Talent Hunt

    An end-to-end talent classification system was developed using the Scoutium dataset. An accuracy of 88% and a precision of 83% were achieved with the LGBM model, showcasing expertise in sports data and advanced machine learning techniques.

    House Price Prediction

    A model for predicting house prices was developed, with an R² score of 0.91 being achieved, indicating high accuracy in price forecasts.

    Customer Churn Analytics

    A project focused on predicting customer churn using CatBoost, applying data science techniques to improve customer retention strategies.

    Diabetes Prediction

    After identifying the features, developed a diabetes prediction model with Random Forest and 74% accuracy, aiding in early identification of at-risk individuals. Before developing the model, necessary data analysis and feature engineering steps were meticulously detailed.

    CLTV Prediction with BG-NBD & Gamma-Gamma Model

    Using the FLO dataset, a comprehensive roadmap was developed to create medium and long-term strategies for sales and marketing activities in the retail sector. Predictive analysis was conducted using analytical techniques such as BG-NBD and Gamma-Gamma models to estimate the potential Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) that existing customers will contribute to the company in the future.

    Customer Segmentation with RFM & Unsupervised Learning

    For the FLO company in the retail sector, customers were segmented using RFM analysis and KMeans in two separate projects. By defining customer behaviors and creating groups based on these behavior clusters, more effective marketing strategies can be developed for each customer segment.

    This approach allows tailored marketing strategies to be implemented, maximizing engagement and satisfaction among different customer groups.

    Comprehensive Data Analysis and Visualization on Streaming Services and Retail Data

    This project consists of two key analyses:

    First, an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was performed on Spotify and YouTube datasets to identify trends and patterns in streaming behaviors, helping to understand user engagement and content popularity

    Second, the Northwind Traders dataset was analyzed using SQL and visualized with Power BI to gain insights into business operations, sales performance, and customer behavior, supporting strategic decision-making and operational improvements.

    The project demonstrates proficiency in data analysis, SQL, and Power BI.

    Hotel Booking Data Analysis and Visualization with Power BI

    This project focused on addressing high cancellation rates and optimizing revenue for hotels.

    The analysis revealed that Travel Agent bookings had a 15% higher cancellation rate compared to direct bookings, and corporate bookings had a 20% higher average daily rate (ADR) than transient ones.

    By creating detailed visualizations and dashboards, the project provided actionable insights into booking patterns and customer behavior, enabling more effective strategies for improving customer retention and maximizing revenue.
